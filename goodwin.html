Project Notes

Goodwin College decided it was time for a homepage refresh. Their old site was a little bare, and did not make effective use of space. The user experience could be improved on mobile and on desktop.

We discussed the homepage and what they wanted to see. They were most interested in highlighting their Degree Programs and their continuing professional education offerings, while introducing new content in the form of Student Profiles. They also wanted a section for news, and some quick links for their users. Check out the live site.
