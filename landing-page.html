Project Notes

After researching best practices for maximizing conversions, I designed a new template for Drexel's marketing landing page. It includes an engaging headline and call to action, along with a new look for the form.

These landing pages are in use for many of the campaigns that run for various colleges and entities at the University. The new design moves more content above the fold and includes space for clients to highlight their programs.
